HDF5 and Nexus Files



HDF5 with the file extension 'h5' and Nexus files 'nxs', which are most usefully encoded in HDF5 format, are supported by the workbench. The viewer has two pages, the first containing the data sets which may be plotted, viewed and sliced as required. The second containing the nexus tree from which elements may be selected and their information viewed in the 'Value' view part.
